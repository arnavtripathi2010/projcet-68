    School Church

    Was established on 6 June 1966

    The school was established by the Mar Thoma Church Educational Society (MTCES) on 6 June 1966. It is located on a 30-acre (120,000 m2) campus, at Mukkolakkal, village on the outskirts of Thiruvananthapuram city.
